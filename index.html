Make your own simple water filter using natural materials!

Apparatus

  • 1 Plastic bottle (cut in half)
  • Sand (fine and coarse)
  • Charcoal (crushed)
  • Gravel or small stones
  • Cotton or coffee filter
  • Muddy water (for testing)

Procedure

Step 1: Prepare the Bottle

Cut the plastic bottle in half. Use the top half as the filter. Turn it upside down like a funnel.

Step 2: Add Cotton

Place cotton or a coffee filter at the neck of the bottle. This stops fine particles.

Step 3: Add Charcoal

Crush charcoal into small pieces and add a 2-inch layer. It removes chemicals and odors.

Step 4: Add Sand

Add a layer of fine sand, followed by coarse sand. Sand helps trap dirt.

Step 5: Add Gravel

Put a layer of gravel or small stones on top. This blocks large debris.

Step 6: Filter the Water

Pour muddy water into the top. Clear water will drip out from the bottom.
